## Who We Are

**TMF Engineerings** is a Karachi-headquartered team of engineers helping university students across Pakistan **design, build, document and understand** their engineering projects — from a single OEL to a full FYDP — and we take on **client/company engineering projects** as well.

No shortcuts, no copy-paste. Every project is built in-house, delivered with a proper report, and explained line-by-line so you can present it as your own work — because you'll actually understand it.
